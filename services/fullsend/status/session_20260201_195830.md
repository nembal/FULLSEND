## Design Decisions

### Why Multi-Repo Targeting?
Broader funnel. CTOs interested in any of these tools likely face similar eng bottleneck problems.

### Why 30-Day Recency?
Tighter intent signal. Someone who starred last week is more likely engaged than 6 months ago.

### Why Follow-Up at Day 5?
Test persistence value. Does a second touch increase meeting rate by 3x+?

### Why "Re:" Subject Line?
Previous experiment showed 8%+ reply rate with this pattern. It breaks inbox filters and feels like continuing a conversation.

### Why Test Phase First?
Send 50 → review sentiment → scale. Avoid spam reputation damage if template is off.
